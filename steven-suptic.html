Steven Suptic - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Nationality, Body Measurement, Career

Steven Suptic is a famous YouTuber and vlogger known for his channel ‘Sugar Pine 7’. Steven Suptic initially started by creating Minecraft-related videos under the name ‘MlgHwnT’.

Steven Suptic is a 28 years old talented personality who was born on February 6, 1993. He is originally from Illinois. Later he moved to California. His zodiac sign is Aquarius. He once featured his father in his YouTube video but didn’t share his name. Furthermore, he has not shared any more information about his childhood, education and family background. Professional Life

Steven began his YouTube career on January 6, 2011, under the name ‘MIgHwnT.’ His channel focused on videos related to Minecraft commentaries and animated videos. It started gaining popularity and even featured collaborations with other YouTubers like ‘AviatorGaming’, ‘SkyDoesMinecraft’, ‘MunchingBrotato’, and ‘BurtGasm’. In 2014, Steven had become one of the more popular YouTube gamers and created a second channel named ‘HWNTwo’, which was not centered on Minecraft. Further, Steven has created an animated series called “To Kill a YouTuber” and “Wild Adventures.”

Likewise, On March 16, 2015, he started his collaboration with Reina Scully on the channel under the direction of Philip DeFranco, the creator of SourceFed’. They began a YouTube channel named ‘Super Panic Frenzy’, which featured gaming videos and other content in English and Japanese. The channel was closed on August 9, 2016. Through this time he had two very popular animated series on YouTube as well, ‘To Kill a YouTuber’ and ‘Wild Adventures’.

Beyond Minecraft

By the end of 2015, he had already got disappointed with Minecraft. Similarly, he had also done almost all kinds of videos related to the game and started focusing on his original vlogs. In 2016, he changed his own channel to ‘The Steven Suptic Experience’. He further began uploading videos on fun challenges and skits. On July 13, 2015, he posted his first video ‘Bro Threesome’ which grew as the most popular video on his rebranded channel with over 1 million views to date.

Moreover, he cast the main host of ‘SourceFed’ in September 2016 due to his great relationship with Philip DeFranco. He became a member of the team until March 25, 2017, when the channel was closed. His ideas, creativity, and hosting abilities made a significant part of making ‘SourceFed’ one of the most popular channels of all time.

In 2017, he started focusing on his own channel which had again been renamed to ‘Sugar Pine 7’. He hired ex-‘SourceFed’ editor Autumn Farrell which made good changes with the quality of his videos on the channel.Some of the most viewed videos on his channel are ‘I hired Autumn, and here’s why’, ‘Sourcefed’s April Fool’s prank’, ‘Just a Couple Friends – Acoustic (Music Video)’.

Other

Further, he also produced the comedy web series ‘Alternative Lifestyle’ which was a hit. He also started doing podcasts on his channel under the name ‘Beyond the Pine’. He featured many of his videos feature other popular YouTubers like ‘ParkerGames’ and ‘KKComics’. Additionally, he is also associated with channels like ‘Rooster Teeth’ and ‘Let’s Play’.

On March 5, 2017, he with longtime friend Reina Scully began the podcast channel ‘Boys Only Club’.

Awards and Nominations

Sugar Pine 7’s Alternative Lifestyle won the Streamy Award for Show of the Year in 2017. They attended RTX Austin in 2017 and attended RTX Sydney in 2018. Further, at the starting of November 2017, they had released their first short film, entitled The Woods, a “cheesy horror story.” Kyle Kizu of The Daily Californian named Sugar Pine 7 “the pinnacle of YouTube content creation.”
