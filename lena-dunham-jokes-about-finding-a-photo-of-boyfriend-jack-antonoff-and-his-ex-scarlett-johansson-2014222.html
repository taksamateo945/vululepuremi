Lena Dunham Jokes About Jack Antonoff, Ex Scarlett Johansson Photo

Wait, is that ScarJo? During an interview with Grantland‘s Bill Simmons, Lena Dunham shared a hilarious story from when she stumbled upon a photo of boyfriend Jack Antonoff with his former girlfriend — Scarlett Johansson.

“The craziest thing that ever happen to me, was like, Old Loves is my passion, I check it like once a week,” the Girls creator, 27, said to Simmons in an interview posted Thursday. “And I was going through it and I saw my boyfriend and his girlfriend from high school.” After noting the ex to be none other than the Avengers actress, she added: “I know I shouldn’t spill that in a public forum, but you can Google it.”

Old Loves is a Tumblr page devoted to photos of celebrity couples who are no longer together. The fun. guitarist dated Johansson, 29, for a year back in 2001 when the pair attended the Professional Children’s School in New York City.
