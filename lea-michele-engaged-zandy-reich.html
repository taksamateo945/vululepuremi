Lea Michele gets engaged to boyfriend Zandy Reich

Lea Michele is engaged!

The 31-year-old actress announced her engagement to boyfriend Zandy Reich.

Michele took to social media, where she showed off her massive engagement ring in a picture alongside the simple caption “yes“.

According to People, Reich proposed to Michele with a four-carat elongated radian cut ring that he personally designed with Leor Yerushalmi and The Jewelers of Las Vegas.

Reich is president of the AYR clothing company. The pair went public with their relationship back in July 2017 but were friends for years before that.

Congratulations Lea and Zandy!
