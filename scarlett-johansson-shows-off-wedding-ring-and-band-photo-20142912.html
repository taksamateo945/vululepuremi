Scarlett Johansson Shows Off Wedding Ring and Band: Photo

Holy carats! Scarlett Johansson’s husband Romain Dauriac didn’t hold back when it came to the star’s engagement ring and wedding band. Johansson, 30, who secretly wed Dauriac this past October, showed off her bling while leaving Beverly Hills eatery Matsuhisa on Sunday, Dec. 28.

As previously reported by Us, the couple secretly said “I do” this past October in a private ceremony officiated by Richard J. Miller at the Ranch at Rock Creek in Philipsburg, Mont. They married just a few weeks after the September birth of daughter Rose Dorothy.
