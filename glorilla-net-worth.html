Glorilla’s Career

In late April 2022, GloRilla seriously contemplated pursuing rap as a career, marking the beginning of her journey in the genre. Her initial foray into rapping surfaced with the track “FNF Let’s Go,” which took a few weeks to gain traction and eventually went viral across social media platforms. A noteworthy aspect is that she recorded both a track and a song on the same day, showcasing her dedication and swift progression.

Using Instagram, she teased her upcoming release by sharing a trailer and a snippet of the track. Notably, DJ Duffy gave the song an in-depth review, contributing to its rising popularity. A TikTok featuring GloRilla’s bars in the background further propelled her into viral fame within days.

Before her breakout, GloRilla had established her presence on SoundCloud, initially sharing rap music under the moniker “party thugs.” Among her releases were tracks like “wear and ride,” along with others titled “solo” and “favorite.” By the end of 2018, she ventured into YouTube and SoundCloud, dropping a rap song called “P states.”

The turning point arrived in 2020 when she collaborated with a producer, leading to the creation of the hit track “To Be Honest.” This collaboration garnered massive attention, with its accompanying video amassing 22 million views on YouTube. GloRilla expanded her repertoire, performing at live concerts and endorsing various fashion brands.

Her recent video, hosted on the YouTube channel “Hitkidd WIDM,” surged to 25 million views within a mere three months, further solidifying her growing presence in the rap scene. Alongside her successful singles, GloRilla ventured into creating her music albums, marking a significant milestone in her burgeoning career.

Earning recognition, she inked deals with several labels, capitalizing on her breakthrough hit “FNF,” which continues to amass a growing global fanbase.
