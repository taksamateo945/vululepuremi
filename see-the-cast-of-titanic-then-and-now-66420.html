Can you believe it’s been 21 years since the epic love story Titanic hit theaters?! We certainly can’t! Back in 1997, the movie’s cast — including Leonardo DiCaprio, Kate Winslet, and more — stole our hearts, and today, we’re taking a look back a the film’s stars then and now. It has been two decades ya know — a lot has changed!

One thing we did learn after all these years, however, is that Kate never had a crush on Leo, which we find to be nearly impossible honestly! “It was seven months of very intense work, we were both really very young. And luckily, and this was the fortunate thing, we never fancied each other! I know that’s really annoying to hear, sorry. We really never did,” the actress previously shared. “So it did mean that we were able to have this… be able to tease each other, which we still do, which is really amazing.”

She added, “The lovely thing about Titanic for me now, my children and their friends… their friends say, ‘Oh I love the bit in Titanic when you did…’ they are reciting lines from Titanic that, I mean, I’ve long forgotten!” Well, we haven’t forgotten a thing — and we can’t wait to watch the movie for the millionth time today in celebration!
