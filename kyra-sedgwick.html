Kyra Sedgwick News - Us Weekly

Name:Kyra Sedgwick

Hometown:New York, NY

Birthday:August 19, 1965

About

Best known for her Golden Globe-winning leading performance on the TNT’s drama The Closer, Sedgwick was born in 1965 in New York.

A graduate of USC, the actress has also appeared as John Travolta‘s love interest in Phenomenon as well as in Something to Talk About, among many other films.

She has been married to actor/singer Kevin Bacon since 1988 and the couple has two children.
