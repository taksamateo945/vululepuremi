Justin Long &amp; Girlfriend Kate Bosworth Go Instagram Official While on Vacation in Ireland

Justin Long and Kate Bosworth are taking a big step in their relationship!

On Tuesday (May 24), the 43-year-old He’s Just Not That Into You actor took to Instagram to share the first photos of he and 39-year-old Blue Crush actress together while on vacation Ireland.

In the selfies, Justin and Kate are both sporting foam mustaches after taking a sip out of the same pint of Guinness beer while out to dinner.

Justin captioned the post with the green heart emoji and Irish flag emoji.

Kate commented on the post, writing, “I’m the luckiest. ?✨?”

If you didn’t know, Justin and Kate met in early 2021 on the set of a movie and have very quietly dating since then.

Earlier this month, the two were spotted showing off some cute PDA during a walk around New York City.

In a recent interview, Justin gushed about being “in love” with Kate!
