John Boyd Biography

John Boyd is one of the most popular and richest War Hero who was born on January 23, 1927 in Pennsylvania, Pennsylvania, United States. After serving in the Korean and Vietnam wars, this American fighter pilot, flight instructor, and military theorist and tactical expert was hired by Major General Arthur C. Agan, Jr. as a high-level advisor and analyst at the U.S. Pentagon.

He and politician Gary Hart both allied themselves with the Defense Reform Movement of the 1970s and ’80s.

A native of Erie, Pennsylvania, he spent the later years of his life in West Palm Beach, Florida. He and his wife, Mary, raised daughters named Kathryn and Mary Ellen and sons named Scott, Jeff, and Stephen.
