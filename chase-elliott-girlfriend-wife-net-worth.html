Who Is Chase Elliott?

Chase Elliott is a stock car racing driver who competes full-time in the NASCAR Cup Series and part-time in the NASCAR Xfinity Series. He drives the No.9 Chevrolet Camaro ZL1 2LE which is an American pony car.

Talking about his accomplishments, Chase won the 2014 NASCAR Nationwide Series championship. Later in 2016, he started racing in the Cup Series on a full-time basis. The same year, he got named the 2016 NASCAR Sprint Cup Series Rookie of the Year.

Eventually, Chase won the NASCAR Cup Series championship in 2020. With that, he now has 13 career wins in the Cup Series, including seven on road courses.

Chase Elliott Bio & Family; Age, Parents, Height

Born as William Clyde Elliott II, Chase is now 25 years of age. He was born on 28 November 1995 in Dawsonville, Georgia, the United States. Chase holds white ethnicity and his birth sign is Sagittarius.

Chase is the son of an American former professional stock car racing driver, Bill Elliott, and his wife, Cindy Elliott. His father, Bill, had a prominent racing career as he won the 1988 Winston Cup Championship and garnered 44 wins in that series.

Talking about his family, Chase shares a sibling bond with his two sisters. His sisters are named Starr Elliott and Brittany Elliott.
